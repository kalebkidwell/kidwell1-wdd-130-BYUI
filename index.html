Alpha Testing Group!

If you are interested in becoming a member of our Alpha Testing group then welcome to the right place!
When you sign up for this service you will recieve our up and coming pairs of EchoBot headphones that are not quite ready
for full shipping, but that sport new ideas and advancements that we have gotten from you and many others of our trusted supporters!
To get started with the sign up process all you need to do is go over to our Contact Us page and scroll down to our comments and
questions section. There you will find a section dedicated to those who would like to be part of this special program! Just put down
name and email address with a request to be part of the Alpha team, and our customer support will get back to you with more inforamtion
about the products you will be reciving, and where to go to keep us up to date with your use of these pre-beta headsets! This and
any other information will be provided by our support team, have fun adventuring!
